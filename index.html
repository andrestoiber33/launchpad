About Us

Launchpad was born from a simple realization: talent is equally distributed globally, but opportunity is not. Navigating international job markets, understanding visa sponsorships, and tailoring applications to different cultural standards is exhausting. We built an AI agent to level the playing field. Whether you're in São Paulo dreaming of Stockholm, or in London looking at Sydney, we believe borders shouldn't dictate your career trajectory.
